Green Smoothie Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup apple juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Ice cubes (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Wash spinach and kale leaves thoroughly.
  2. Place spinach, kale, and banana in a blender.
  3. Add apple juice, water, and ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy your refreshing smoothie!
Quinoa Salad Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the quinoa, cucumber, bell pepper, and onion.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss everything together and serve chilled.
Grilled Chicken Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  4. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
